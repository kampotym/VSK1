# Project Concept & Charter

## Project Title
AI-Based Personal Finance Assistant App

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to develop a simple AI-based personal finance assistant that helps users automatically categorize their financial transactions and generate a monthly spending summary.

The system reduces the time required for manual expense tracking and provides users with a clearer understanding of their spending habits.

## Problem Statement
Many individuals struggle to track their daily expenses and understand where their money is spent. Manual expense tracking is time-consuming and often inaccurate, which leads to poor financial management.

Without proper financial insights, users may overspend or fail to plan their budgets effectively.

## Justification
With the increasing number of digital transactions, users require automated tools that analyze their spending patterns.

An AI-based finance assistant can automatically categorize transactions and generate spending summaries, helping users make better financial decisions.

## Expected Users
The expected users include:

- university students
- young professionals
- freelancers
- individuals who want to manage personal expenses more efficiently
